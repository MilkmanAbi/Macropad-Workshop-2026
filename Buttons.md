## Button Layout

</div>

**Layer 0 — Clipboard** `LED: Cyan`
| BTN1 | BTN2 | BTN3 |
|------|------|------|
| Copy | Paste | Cut |

**Layer 1 — Media** `LED: Purple`
| BTN1 | BTN2 | BTN3 |
|------|------|------|
| Vol Up | Vol Down | Play/Pause |

**Layer 2 — Communication** `LED: Orange`
| BTN1 | BTN2 | BTN3 |
|------|------|------|
| Mic Toggle | Speaker Mute | Cam Toggle |

> Long press BTN1+BTN2 on this layer to cycle platform: **Teams → Zoom → Discord** (LED blinks 2/3/4 times to confirm)

**Layer 3 — Coding** `LED: Red`
| BTN1 | BTN2 | BTN3 |
|------|------|------|
| Save | Undo | Redo |

**Layer 4 — Roll** `LED: Rainbow`
| BTN1 | BTN2 | BTN3 |
|------|------|------|
| Open Browser | Secret | Secret |

**Layer 5 — Custom** `LED: Mint (default)`
> Configured via `custom.conf` — no Python needed. See file for options.

**Layer switching:** Short press BTN1+BTN2 together → next layer

## 🎮 Secret Game Mode

</div>

Press **all three buttons at once** to trigger a hidden Simon Says memory game.

- **BTN1 = Red · BTN2 = Green · BTN3 = Blue**
- Watch the LED flash a sequence, then repeat it in order
- 3 rounds of increasing length
- Pass all 3 rounds to claim your prize 👀
